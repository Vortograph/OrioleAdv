Who are the Oriole Advocates?

The Oriole Advocates are residents, business owners, and other concerned citizens who believe that strong public schools are critical to the area’s growth and success. We aim to build an aligned vision among the commUNITY that makes us AVON STRONG. To this end:

We abstain from and do not support purposely divisive statements or language intended to disparage those with differing views.
We engage in helpful, respectful communication that supports ACSC’s students, staff, and administrators — bolstering their efforts to remain a leading education institution in Indiana.
We seek common ground to help bind the schools and the community together more tightly.
